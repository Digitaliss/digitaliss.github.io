Mimicry

 What is Mimicry?:

  • Mimicry is a two player 3D game of deception and perception.
  • Players play as a mimic and as a farmer.
  • The mimic must blend in with the livestock animals on the farm, while trying to eat all the animals.
  • The farmer is the owner of this farmland and must find and kill the mimic before all the animals are killed.
  • Final team project of COMP 476: Advanced Game Development, Concordia University.
  • Created by Jennifer Sunahara, Eric Davies, Sabrina D'Mello, Pierre-André Léger, Jordan Senosiain
  • Powered by Unity3D and Photon.
