12Bis

 What is 12Bis?:

  • Two player game based on communication that targets a teenage audience.
  • Contains puzzles and riddles that must be solved by communicating with the other player.
  • Creates pervasive gameplay that can lead to urban exploration in the real world.
  • Created by team Lumina: Elaine Gusella, Alizé Rey, Marie-Pier Bouffard, Véro Ax, Katryne Laberge and Jennifer Sunahara. Sound by Amélie Bouita.
  • Selected out of 18 games for the Judges Choice Award in 2016 during the Ubisoft Creative Jam.
  • Powered by Unity3D. Created with Fungus.
  • PLAY Player 0ne!
