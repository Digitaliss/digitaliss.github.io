Cateteria

 What is Cateteria?:

  • Cateteria is an original twist on a single player space shooter.
  • Players play as a pilot in a spaceship defending a magical pizza planet from evil capitalist drones
  • Planet makes delicious magical pizzas made by the best good cat-chefs.
  • Player can make friends with cat wizards to cast laser-spells at the capitalists.
  • Created by 4 Pro Skaters members: Jennifer Sunahara, Eleanor Jacques-Morel, Sarah Shin, Anna De Cicco.
  • Powered by Unity3D.
